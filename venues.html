Stewart Center

Note: The east entrance of Stewart Center is located immediately west of the Purdue Memorial Union. Enter via this entrance, and take the stairs on the right to the third floor.

Address: 128 Memorial Mall, West Lafayette, IN 47907

Parking (for both Stewart Center and PMU): During the weekend, parking in the Wood Street Parking Garage is free. The garage is just south of the SE corner of Grant St. and State St., while the Union is located at the NW corner of the intersection.
When approaching from the east on State Street, turn left onto S. Grant and the garage entrance will be on your left.
Map to garage: https://goo.gl/maps/m4iQ5PKo9H82

France A. Cordova Recreation Center ("CoRec")

Note: Access to the CoRec is restricted to authorized members. Please inform the staff at the check-in kiosk that you are with Whistle Stop to obtain entry.

Address: 355 N Martin Jischke Dr, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Map: https://goo.gl/maps/xHVHyoMNC3T2

Parking: On weekends, parking in the CoRec parking lot is free. The parking lot can be accessed from Stadium Ave, just west of Martin Jischke Drive.
Parking Lot Entrance (roughly): https://goo.gl/maps/2hYUiQH6XVL2

Merou Grotto

Address: 1860 N River Rd, West Lafayette, IN 47906

Map: https://goo.gl/maps/WXuibN4WQtz

Parking: Merou Grotto parking is located immediately in front of the venue; please note, however, that there are limited spots. Carpooling, or use of the shuttle from the PMU, are highly encouraged.
